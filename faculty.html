Vicki Marcus

For more than 35 years, Vicki Marcus, our COO and BMoreFit instructor, has been inspiring and motivating people to get in shape. With national certifications ranging from group fitness to personal training, Vicki uses her lifelong teaching skills to motivate and energize children and adults across the country. She is the creator and President of Fizz Kidz, Inc.

Jeff Thompson

Jeff Thompson is a Nationally Certified Personal Trainer & former Division 1 athlete who has dedicated his life to seeing healthier communities. This passion has been displayed through different fitness opportunities offered to inner-city youth such as: sports camps, fitness classes, and mentoring relationships. He is excited about being on the B-more fit team, serving youth and their families by empowering them to live active, healthy lives. Jeff has a vision of seeing Baltimore be known as the healthiest city in America, and seeing kids become the change agents that lead to whole families choosing to live healthy, physically fit lives.

Heather LNTK

Heather is a certified Personal Trainer and Certified Group Fitness Instructor by the American Council on Exercise (ACE). She works as a Personal Trainer for FX Studios at the Under Armour Combine Training Center. She has more than 8 years of experience in fitness training and instruction and holds two youth specific certifications- Youth Fitness Specialist from ACE and Crossfit Kids certification. Heather is currently a Head Coach for the first Girls on the Run program in Baltimore.
