© Open Society Institute

BMoreFit was founded by Jon Kaplan, “Baltimore’s Best Personal Trainer” voted on by Baltimore Magazine. As an Open Society Institute fellow, he developed innovative strategies to improve fitness in communities with widespread obesity.

Vicki Marcus

For more than 35 years, Vicki Marcus, our Executive Director, Program Director and BMoreFit coach, has been inspiring and motivating people to get in shape. With national certifications ranging from group fitness to personal training, Vicki uses her lifelong teaching skills to motivate and energize children and adults across the country. She is the creator and President of Fizz Kidz, Inc.
