Tribute Cards

Send beautiful BMoreFit Contribution Cards to your friends and family for any occasion. What a great way to acknowledge a special occasion AND get every child eating better, living well, and moving more!

Tribute Cards are for all occasions…

  • Birthday
  • Anniversary
  • Mother’s Day
  • Father’s Day
  • Graduation
  • Special Milestone
  • Birth of a Child
  • Christmas
  • Chanukah
  • Valentine’s Day
  • In Memory of a Loved One

Choose from one of the convenient options below to make a gift in honor of, in memory of, or wishing good health to someone you care about. We will send a card acknowledging your thoughtfulness. The minimum gift is $15.

ONE card:
$15. You can send it – or we can send it.
Do It Yourself Pack:
Save 33% and purchase 5 cards for $50. You purchase a pack of 5 cards to complete and send from the comfort of your own home as needed.
Do It Yourself Bonus Pack:
Save 45% and purchase 12 cards for $100. You choose your own style of cards and we will mix and match to give your exactly what you need.
Easy Account:
One quick call is all it takes. Contact our office today about setting up a tribute card account to make sending tribute cards easier than ever. You send BMoreFit a check; we create an account and send a card whenever you need us to. Set yours up today!
