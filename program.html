BMoreFit Fit Kit for Teachers

BMoreFit creates a healthy school culture that enables children to be educated on making good choices regarding nutrition, health and wellness.

  • Weekly Coach Led Fitness Breaks
  • Weekly Communication between BMoreFit Coach and Teachers
  • Integrated Fitness Curriculum
  • Weekly Fitness Announcements
  • School-wide Monthly Newsletters
  • Family Fitness Programs
  • Fitness Assemblies
  • Wellness Committees
  • BMoreFit Toolkits

BMoreFit is the expert on transforming fitness into fun while providing support in learning. BMoreFit coaches establish a weekly schedule of classroom fitness breaks which introduce kids to dance, movement, balance and flexibility, endurance and pure ENERGY!
