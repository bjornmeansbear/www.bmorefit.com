Testimonials

It is with pleasure that I write this letter of support to congratulate and promote the Baltimore Fitness Academy (BMoreFit) and its signature initiative, the Teacher ToolKit. We have found that there are many benefits of teachers using the toolkit in their classrooms to facilitate fitness breaks throughout the school day. Regular use of the fitness deck and videos increased our students’ physical activity; improved their general health and conditioning; enhanced their focus and attention; and in some small measure may have even influenced academic performance. You and your team of educators and physical fitness trainers have created a valuable resource; one that should be implemented in every classroom and in every school.

—Edward E. Cozzolino
Principal
Green Street Academy

BMoreFit was an entirely positive experience for my students. It helped build community in bringing out shy students and giving the students a common activity they enjoyed. The students loved doing Fizz Kidz at home with their families, and had the lines and moves memorized. The fitness cards allowed me to complete the workouts without technology if need be. The students loved when Vicki visited our classroom and she became our most cherished and consistent visitor. I had no other volunteers that made multiple appearances in the classroom so she really meant a lot to my students. The workouts gave the students an outlet to release energy productively and I had no behavior issues during that time. I would often use the fitness breaks as incentives which also helped with classroom management. Overall I appreciate that I was offered the program free of cost and would definitely want to be included next year.

Holabird
